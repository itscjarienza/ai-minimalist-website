Who Should Apply

The AI audit is the right next step if:

  • You have a team of 10 to 50 people running repeatable internal processes
  • You've spent money on AI tools without seeing a clear return
  • Your workflows feel inefficient but you're not sure exactly where the problem is
  • You want an outside expert to look at your operation and give you a direct prescription

The audit is not the right fit if you're looking for a list of tools to test, or if your processes aren't yet defined enough to map. In that case, start with the free diagnostic.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with The AI Minimalist.

How much does the audit cost?

Pricing is discussed during the application review. The audit is a premium engagement — it is not a cheap consultation. If cost is the primary concern, start with the free diagnostic.

Do I need to prepare anything before the first session?

No formal preparation required. It helps to come with a specific workflow in mind that feels inefficient or unclear, but we can find that together if needed.

What if I've already tried AI tools and they didn't work?

That's the most common starting point. Tools that don't work usually don't work because of placement — not because the technology is bad. The audit identifies exactly where the placement broke down.

Is this only for tech-forward businesses?

No. The methodology was built on industrial operations experience, not software. If your team does repeatable work — regardless of industry — the audit applies.

What happens after the audit?

You receive a documented system map and a prioritized prescription. From there, you can implement on your own, hire your own team, or continue into Implementation Consulting with The AI Minimalist.
