06 — Style Rules
Logomark Usage
Always use the Convergence mark. Three lines collapse into a single ringed node. Never stretch, rotate, or recolor outside the defined palette. Minimum size: 16px wide.
Typography
Fraunces is always 400 — never bold. Epilogue body at 300. Labels tracked at +0.12–0.2em. No italics in UI contexts. Headings never exceed 48px in body content.
Color
Warm Cream (#ede2c7) is the default light background. Dark (#242424) is the default dark. Cream Yellow (#f0f8be) is an accent — use sparingly. Never use more than 3 palette colors in a single composition.
Spacing
Base unit: 8px. All spacing in multiples — 8, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 80. Generous white space is a brand signal. Never crowd the mark or wordmark.
Lines & Borders
Dividers at 1px only. Never thicker. Use opacity (20–40%) to soften. Square corners throughout — no border radius. The brand is thin-line, not thick-line.
Voice Alignment
Direct. Diagnostic. Confident without being loud. Short sentences. No filler. The brand never screams — it states. Every word earns its place, same as every tool in the system.
